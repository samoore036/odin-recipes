Description

Easy one pan Spanish chicken and rice. Made with simple seasonings that come together with zesty, bold flavors in this one pot, 30 minute meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 2 1/4 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 lemon
  • chopped cilantro or parsley, if cilantro tastes like soap to you (as garnish)
  • Spanish seasoning mix

Steps

  1. Make seasoning
  2. Make chicken
  3. Add rice, broth, juice from the lemon
  4. Cook until absorbed and rice is tender
